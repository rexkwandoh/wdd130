Home page

Hello, I'm Austin Eaquinto. I live in Rexburg, Idaho with my wife and kids. I enjoy spending time with my family or alone, working on my pet projects. I've worked in food service, both fast food and restaraunt. I've also worked as an auto mechanic for a few years, I even did a short gig as a receptionist for a post acute care center. Currently I am a stay at home Dad while I pursue my Bachelor's Degree in Software Engineering. My current studies are my first look into the computing world and it has been a satisfying and enjoyable experience. The following are to give you an idea of the kind of person I am. I enjoy organization and respect timelines. Figuring out a problem with my hands or with a pencil and paper might as well be a hobby because I find them so fun. I strive to give my best effort in my work and to be honest in all of my interactions and dealings with people. I am confident in my knowledge like to push myself to learn as much as I can. As you peruse my website you will find projects that I have worked on and my personal contact information. Please look through my work and if you like what you see I look forward to hearing from you.
